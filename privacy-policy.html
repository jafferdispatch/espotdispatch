Privacy Policy

At Espot Dispatch, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website www.Espot Dispatch.com.

Personal Information We Collect

  • When you submit a form on our website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any additional information you provide.

  • When you visit our website, we may also collect information about your device, IP address, and browsing behavior through Google Analytics. This information is used to improve our website and provide a better user experience.

    • How We Use Your Information

  • We use the information you provide to contact you regarding your inquiry or to send you promotional materials related to our services.

  • We may also use your information to improve our website and provide better customer service.

    • How We Protect Your Information

  • We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • We store your personal information on secure servers and use SSL encryption technology to protect your data when transmitting it over the internet.

    • Cookies

  • Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to collect information about how our website is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to have them stored on your device.

    • Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any significant changes and provide you with the updated policy on our website.

    • Contact Us

  • If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at contact@espotdispatch.com.
